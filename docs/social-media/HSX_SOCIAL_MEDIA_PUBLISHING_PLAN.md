# HSX DIECASTING Social Media Publishing Plan

## 1. Purpose

HSX social media is a B2B trust-building system, not an entertainment account or random advertising channel.

## 2. Platform Roles

- LinkedIn: main B2B trust and customer development channel.
- YouTube: factory process evidence library.
- Facebook: secondary brand presence and later precise group observation.
- Instagram: visual factory/process display.
- TikTok: later short-video testing channel, not the current priority.

## 3. Content Pillars

- Real factory process
- OEM aluminum die casting project review
- Customer pain points and solutions
- Main product directions: OEM aluminum die casting parts, die-cast aluminum LED light housing parts, aluminum housings/enclosures, semi-finished casting blanks, secondary machining and surface finishing
- Practical factory experience

## 4. Recommended Publishing Frequency for the Early Stage

- LinkedIn: 1 high-quality post per week first, later up to 2 per week if stable.
- YouTube: 1 real factory/process video every 7 to 14 days.
- Facebook: 1 simplified post per week, adapted from LinkedIn or YouTube.
- Instagram: 1 image/Reel per week when suitable visual material is available.
- TikTok: test later, no more than 1 short video per week in the beginning.

## 5. First 4-Week Content Direction

### Week 1

- YouTube: first mold maintenance video, already published.
- LinkedIn: first mold maintenance factory experience post, already published.

### Week 2

- RFQ checklist: what buyers should send for faster project review.

### Week 3

- Why product application matters before tooling.

### Week 4

- Die-cast aluminum LED light housing project review: heat dissipation, installation, surface finishing, and batch production stability.

## 6. Post-Publishing Operation Loop

After every LinkedIn / YouTube / Facebook / Instagram / TikTok post:

- Check first 1 to 3 hours for likes, comments, views, followers, company visits, or profile visits.
- Check again after 24 hours.
- Reply to comments professionally and naturally.
- Do not hard sell.
- Do not send mass messages.
- Review whether interacting users are related to LED lighting, OEM manufacturing, aluminum parts, industrial products, purchasing, engineering, or product development.
- Record relevant interactions as potential leads when appropriate.

## 7. Safety and Brand Rules

Do not publish:

- RealMod content
- C-end retail language
- Sexy car-ad style material on the HSX DIECASTING factory account
- Functional brake performance claims for decorative caliper appearance covers
- Customer drawings
- Customer logos
- Customer names
- Internal quotation logic
- Tooling fee or prototype fee bottom-line rules
- Yield or internal quality data
- Full customer development SOP
- Sensitive mold or process details

## 8. Channel Separation

HSX DIECASTING factory channels should focus on aluminum die casting, OEM parts, LED light housing, machining, surface finishing, factory process, and B2B project review.

HSX Caliper Covers content should remain separate and only describe decorative brake caliper appearance covers safely.

## 9. Current Operating Rule

- Do not post multiple new items on the same day during the early stage.
- Do not boost posts yet.
- Do not run ads yet.
- Do not mass-add contacts.
- Do not spam groups.
- Focus on steady trust-building and precise interaction.
