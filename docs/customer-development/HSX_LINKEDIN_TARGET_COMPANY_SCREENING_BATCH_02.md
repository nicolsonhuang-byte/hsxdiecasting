## HSX Positioning Used for Screening

HSX DIECASTING is a real aluminum die casting factory focused on:

- Small-to-medium aluminum die casting parts.
- OEM aluminum components.
- Pump body / pump housing aluminum die casting parts.
- Aluminum housings and enclosures.
- Secondary machining after casting, such as drilling, tapping, and CNC finishing when required.
- Surface finishing support.
- Ordinary commercial and industrial aluminum die casting projects.

## A/B/C Rating Standard

- **A = strong potential buyer:** physical product manufacturer with visible pump, housing, enclosure, or industrial component direction that may need small-to-medium aluminum die casting or related secondary processing.
- **B = observe:** relevant product category, but buyer fit is not yet clear; may be a large manufacturer, established brand, distributor-driven product maker, or company with internal supply.
- **C = skip:** poor fit for HSX outreach because it is mainly a peer service/factory, CNC-only/sheet-metal-only shop, installer, repair/service company, sensitive-use supplier, unrelated retailer, or otherwise not an early HSX target.
