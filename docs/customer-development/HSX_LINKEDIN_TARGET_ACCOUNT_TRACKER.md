## HSX Fit Standard

HSX DIECASTING should prioritize companies with possible needs for suitable small-to-medium OEM aluminum die casting parts, die-cast aluminum LED light housing parts, aluminum housings/enclosures, secondary machining, drilling/tapping, polishing, powder coating, painting, and project-based finishing support.

HSX should not position itself as a large structural die casting supplier, EV giga casting supplier, all-products trading company, or C-end retail brand.

## A/B/C Rating Rules

- **A class:** Highly relevant. Company has stronger evidence such as LED fixture manufacturer, outdoor LED fixture manufacturer, industrial LED fixture manufacturer, LED flood light manufacturer, LED street light manufacturer, die-cast aluminum light housing, aluminum lighting housing, aluminum lamp body, heat sink housing, OEM/ODM lighting products with possible aluminum housing needs, or visible product lines that likely use aluminum housings or die-cast parts.
- **B class:** Possible fit. Company has industrial products, hardware, equipment, or manufacturing-related products, but aluminum die casting needs are not yet clear.
- **C class:** Low priority. Pure retail, unrelated products, competitor/supplier benchmark only, unclear business, or no visible fit with HSX capabilities.

Downgrade or avoid companies mainly focused on lighting installation, solar/energy installation, retrofit services, public lighting project contractors, light poles or steel structures only, SMT/electronics only without visible housing demand, retail display lighting only, or Chinese lighting manufacturers / peer competitors.

## Suggested Human Review Process

1. Open A-class company pages manually and follow only if the page is clearly relevant.
2. Observe recent posts for product launches, sourcing pain points, outdoor lighting projects, aluminum housing mentions, machining, coating, or OEM/private-label signals.
3. Identify only relevant roles before any future connection request: owner, founder, sourcing manager, purchasing manager, product manager, engineering manager, R&D manager, operations manager.
4. Do not send mass messages.
5. Do not pitch decorative brake caliper appearance covers to lighting or enclosure prospects.
6. If future outreach is considered, keep the message factory-real and project-specific: drawings, application, quantity, machining, finishing, packaging, and suitable project review.
