# HSX DIECASTING SEO/GEO Playbook

This document summarizes the current SEO/GEO positioning, public wording rules, and conversion rules for the HSX DIECASTING B2B factory website.

## 1. Core Positioning

- HSX DIECASTING is a real aluminum die casting factory, not a trading company.
- HSX focuses on suitable small-to-medium OEM aluminum die casting projects.
- Do not position HSX as a large structural die casting factory, EV giga casting supplier, or all-products trading company.
- Decorative brake caliper appearance covers are only one decorative, non-load-bearing product category.

## 2. Target Market Direction

- U.S. and Canada are priority English markets.
- Focus on direct factory RFQ, suitable projects, and B2B OEM buyers.
- Avoid platform-price-competition positioning.

## 3. Core Product Priorities

1. OEM Aluminum Die Casting Parts
2. Die Cast Aluminum LED Light Housings
3. Secondary Machining and Surface Finishing
4. Decorative Brake Caliper Appearance Covers
5. Motorcycle / Automotive LED Lighting Products

## 5. Public Wording Rules

Do not publish:

- Fixed yield rates
- Return-rate figures
- Customer names
- 97%
- 50,000 cycles
- Full tooling fee refund promises
- Automatic prototype fee refund promises
- Customer conflict details
- Alibaba or Made-in-China criticism
- EV giga casting positioning

Use cautious wording:

- Suitable projects
- May review
- According to project requirements
- Agreed project terms
- Customer-specific inspection requirements
- Cooperating partners when required

## 6. Factory Experience Themes

Use these themes when building future SEO/GEO and conversion content:

- Right-sized machine tonnage
- Stable mold cavity layout
- Mold success is not only forming the rough casting
- Prototype confirmation before tooling
- We review before we produce
- Stable batch production control
- In-house drilling and tapping
- In-house polishing
- In-house powder coating and painting
- Anodizing through cooperating partners for suitable projects
- Passivation or other project-specific protective surface treatment through cooperating treatment partners when required
- In-house mold maintenance after production

## 7. Conversion Rules

- Get a Quote / Send RFQ should go to `/contact`.
- Project Review should go to `/b2b-oem-project-review`.
- WhatsApp links should only be used where the button clearly says WhatsApp.
- Contact/RFQ pages should tell buyers to send drawings, 3D files, sample photos, application, quantity, material, machining, finishing, and packaging requirements.

## 8. Review Checklist for Future Codex Tasks

Before pushing any future HSX website change, check:

- No overclaims
- No internal commercial rules published
- No fixed quality numbers
- No trading-company positioning
- No caliper-cover-first positioning
- Build passes
- Changed files are reported
- Live pages are checked after deployment
